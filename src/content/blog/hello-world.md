> This is my website. There are many like it, but this one is mine.

I've been meaning to do this for ages. Somewhere that's mine, somewhere that I can put thoughts to text in some kind of persistent way, in my style and on my terms.

The purposes are simple:

- Rubber duck about problems or projects I'm working on.
- Keep notes I might want to refer back to.
- Occasionally write something a bit more polished if I have something to say.

I kept the site design simple, because that's what works for me, and it's what I like to read when I go to other sites.

Thanks for stopping by. The [about](/about) page says a bit more about who I am, and the [blog](/blog) is where everything will land. Given that you're reading this, I suspect you've figured out the latter already.
