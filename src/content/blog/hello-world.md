I've meant to do this for years: a small, plain, durable place to write things
down that isn't owned by a platform that might pivot, paywall, or disappear.
This is that place.

The goals are modest. I want somewhere to:

- think out loud about problems I'm working on,
- keep notes I'll want to find again, and
- occasionally write something longer when an idea earns it.

No analytics theater, no newsletter pop-up, no infinite scroll. Just text, set
well enough that it's actually pleasant to read.

> The best time to start writing was years ago. The second best time is in a
> plain text file, right now.

If you're reading this, thanks for stopping by. The [about](/about) page says a
bit more about who I am, and the [blog](/blog) is where everything will land.
More soon.
